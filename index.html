How It Works

A Practical Path from Knowledge to Digital Asset

A digital asset is more than a file. It has a defined purpose, audience, ownership, version, access level, publication path and improvement plan.

01

Discover

Identify knowledge, experience, research or creative work worth preserving and sharing.

02

Structure

Turn scattered material into a clear framework, workflow, curriculum or content system.

03

Create

Build the smallest useful version: a guide, worksheet, ebook, audio, course or data resource.

04

Register

Record the asset, source files, rights, version, access level, owner and development status.

05

Publish

Release to a suitable channel, test with real users and observe what creates value.

06

Improve

Use feedback to update, combine, license, translate or develop new derivative assets.
